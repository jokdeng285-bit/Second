How Spark works

From sign-up to your first date — here's exactly what to expect on Spark.

1

Build your real profile

Answer 8 quick prompts about your values, hobbies, and what you're looking for. Add photos, link your Spotify for a taste of your music taste, and write a bio that's actually you. No one-line bios allowed.

🎵 Spotify linked 📸 Photo verified ✍️ 8 prompts
2

Get interest-matched daily

Every day at 9 AM, Spark delivers up to 15 curated matches. Our algorithm considers 47 compatibility factors — far beyond age and distance. You'll see exactly why each person was matched to you.

⚡ Daily matches 🎯 47 factors 🧾 Match reasoning
3

Connect without the cringe

When you both like each other, it's a match. Spark suggests three personalised icebreakers so you never stare at a blank message box. Chat freely, and when the vibe is right, move to a voice note or video call — all inside the app.

💬 Icebreakers 🎙️ Voice notes 📹 Video chat
4

Plan the perfect first date

Use Date Night Planner to find activities you'd both love, from escape rooms to vegan tasting menus. Share your location only when you're ready — and always tell a friend where you're going.

📅 Date planner 🗺️ Local suggestions 🔒 Safe sharing
